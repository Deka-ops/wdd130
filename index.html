Abia

Abia is a state in the south-east geopolitical zoone of Nigeria. The state is bordered to the northwest by Anambra state and to the northwest by the states of Enugu and Ebonyi, Imo state to the west, cross River state to the east, Akwa Ibom state to the southeast and Rivers State to the south. Abia is the only Southeastern state that has boundaries with the other four Southeastern states in Nigeria. It takes its name from the acronym of the state's most populated regions: Aba, Bende, Isiukwuato, and Afikpo. The state capital is Umuahia while the largest city and commercial center is Aba.
